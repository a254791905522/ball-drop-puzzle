Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data Collection

Ball Drop Puzzle does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. The game is a fully offline single-player experience with no user accounts, no login, and no network communication.

No Third-Party Services

Ball Drop Puzzle does not include any advertising SDKs, analytics tools, tracking frameworks, social login services, or in-app purchase systems. There are no third-party components that access user data.

Local Data Storage

Game progress, unlocked levels, best star ratings, audio settings, and haptics preferences are stored locally on the device using the standard iOS UserDefaults system. This data never leaves the device and is not accessible to any external party.

Children's Privacy

Ball Drop Puzzle is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). No personal data is collected from any user, including children under 13. There are no features that allow communication with other users.

Data Security

All game data remains on the user's iOS device and is protected by the device's built-in security features. No data is transmitted over the internet or stored on external servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Ball Drop Puzzle App Store product page.
